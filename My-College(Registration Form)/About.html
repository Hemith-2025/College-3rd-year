Our Story

Journey of Excellence

The Capital city of Karnataka - Bangalore, with it's pleasant climate, friendly atmosphere and religious and language tolerance, has captured the heart of many people and has resulted in a population which is a healthy pot-pourri of different cultures and religions.

The Dragon Education Society established in 1947, in Bangalore with an ambition to promote and disseminate knowledge and serve in general the cause of education. The Society has been continuously aiming at developing quality human resources and maintaining high academic standards, covering large segments of the student community.

The relentless pursuit of excellence by Our Team, under the leadership of our Chairman, Dragon Education Society, has led us to strive for quality in all activities. The Quality Improvement Programs and Support programs have provided the students with intellectual depth and training, necessary to pursue productive careers in their professions.
